Terminal Keeper

Elevate your terminal experience!

Effortlessly configuration, seamlessly restore your last session, and manage sessions with ease. Personalize your workspace with colorful themes and boost productivity by importing commands swiftly.

🎉

Session Management

Easily add, edit or delete sessions, keeping your workspace tidy.

⚡

Commands Importing

Quickly import commands from files like package.json, pipenv, Makefile, grunt, gradle, gulp, ant, and more.

⏮

Easy Configuration

Quickly create templates to simplify your terminal setup.

📄

Restore Last Session Automatically

Start your day smoothly by bringing back your last terminal session when you open the app.

🐚

Session Saving (Coming Soon)

Preserve your current terminal session along with its icon, color, and all terminal configurations.

🌈

Customizable Themes

Personalize your terminal with a variety of colorful themes for icons and colors that brighten your workspace.
